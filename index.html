Podfast

A Ruby on Rails Progressive Web Application for mobile, which helps people on the go find and choose podcast episodes quickly. Podfast has improved search preference flexibility, and includes episode likes, listens and friend recommendations. Built with Spotify Integration, an in-app social platform and play or 'Pod'listing.

Browse

The home page of the app, where users can find the most popular podcasts and new releases.

Users also have the option of clicking a button to pick a random episode and can see episodes their friends have listened to
